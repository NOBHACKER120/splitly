Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Splitly collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website, applications, and related services.

By accessing or using Splitly, you agree to the practices described in this policy. This policy applies to all visitors, registered users, and anyone who interacts with our services.

Information We Collect

We collect certain information to provide, improve, and secure our services. This information may be provided directly by you or collected automatically when you use Splitly.

Information You Provide

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Group names and participant details
  • Expense details, amounts, and descriptions
  • Any information you submit through forms or inputs on our platform

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • Browser type and version
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Usage data such as pages visited and actions taken
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect only to provide and improve Splitly. Your data helps us deliver core features, keep the platform secure, and communicate important updates.

  • To create and manage groups and shared expenses
  • To calculate, split, and display expenses accurately
  • To save your activity and preferences across devices
  • To improve app performance, usability, and reliability
  • To communicate important updates, changes, or support messages
  • To detect misuse, prevent fraud, and maintain platform security

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly and provide a better user experience.

  • To keep you logged in and maintain session security
  • To remember your preferences and settings
  • To understand how users interact with Splitly and improve performance
  • To support analytics and future features such as ads or integrations

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect certain features and limit how well Splitly works for you.

Data Sharing & Third-Party Services

We respect your privacy and are committed to keeping your data safe. Splitly does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

We may share limited information only with trusted third-party services that help us operate and improve Splitly. These services are required to handle your data securely and only for necessary purposes.

  • Hosting and infrastructure providers that store and deliver our services
  • Analytics tools that help us understand usage and improve performance
  • Security services used to protect against fraud and abuse

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information and keep it secure. Splitly uses modern security practices designed to safeguard your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss.

  • Data is stored on secure servers with controlled access
  • Encryption is used where appropriate to protect sensitive information
  • Access to user data is limited to authorized personnel only
  • Regular monitoring helps us detect and prevent potential security issues

While we work hard to protect your data, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure. We encourage users to take basic precautions such as keeping login details private.

Your Rights & Choices

You have control over your personal information. Splitly respects your rights and provides ways for you to access, update, or remove your data.

  • Access the personal information associated with your account
  • Edit or update your profile and group information
  • Request deletion of your account and related data
  • Opt out of non-essential emails or notifications
  • Request a copy of your data for personal use

Some requests may need to be handled manually. If you need help with accessing or deleting your data, you can contact us at splitlyofficial@gmail.com.

Children’s Privacy

Splitly is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information on Splitly, please contact us and we will take steps to remove such information from our systems.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, features, or legal requirements.

Any updates will be posted on this page, and the “Last updated” date at the top will be revised accordingly. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you can contact us using the details below.
