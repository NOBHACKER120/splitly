Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how Splitly handles and protects your information. We value transparency and respect your digital privacy.

Effective Date: June 15, 2025

Welcome to Splitly (https://splitly.fun). Your privacy is important. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. What We Collect

  • Email address (if you sign up)
  • Basic usage stats via analytics tools
  • Group or expense info you manually enter

We do NOT collect: sensitive personal data, financial/banking data, or location data.

2. How We Use It

  • To run the Splitly features you use
  • To understand and improve our site
  • To contact you (only if you’ve opted in)

3. Data Storage

We use Firebase (by Google) to securely store login and group data. Data is encrypted and only accessible to you.

4. Cookies

We use basic cookies for app preferences. You can block these in your browser.

5. Your Choices

  • You can use Splitly without signing in
  • You can request data deletion anytime (email: splitlyofficial@gmail.com)

6. Changes

We may update this policy. If it’s a major change, we’ll notify you here.
