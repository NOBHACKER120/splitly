Our Mission

At Splitly, we simplify group finances so friends, roommates, and travelers can share expenses fairly and transparently. No confusion, no awkward reminders—just clear balances and stress-free sharing.

  • Problem we solve: Split bills fairly and avoid misunderstandings.
  • How we help: Easy, transparent, and reliable expense tracking.
  • Core principles: Trust, simplicity, and accuracy.
  • Vision: Empower groups to manage money effortlessly, anywhere in the world.

Our Story

Splitly began with a simple idea: make sharing expenses among friends and roommates easy, fair, and stress-free. Frustrated with messy spreadsheets and forgotten payments, we wanted a solution that anyone could use instantly.

Over time, Splitly has grown into a reliable platform helping thousands of users split bills transparently—whether it's a road trip, monthly rent, or daily coffee runs.

  • 2023: Splitly founded with a vision to simplify group finances.
  • 2024: First 1,000 users onboarded, expanding across multiple countries.
  • 2025: Added new features like instant settlements and group dashboards.

What We Offer

Splitly provides everything you need to manage group expenses smoothly, fairly, and transparently.

  • Bill splitting tools – instantly divide expenses among friends or roommates
  • Group expense tracking – monitor all group payments in one place
  • Balance calculation – know who owes what at any time
  • Multi-device access – seamless experience on web and mobile
  • Ongoing improvements – we constantly add new features based on user feedback
